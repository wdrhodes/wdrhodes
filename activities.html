Activities at Pacific Trails Reosrt

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a State Park.
You can hike the trails alone or join one of our excellent guided hikes.

Kayaking

For the more adventerous why not rent one of our Ocean Kayaks that are available for guests!

Bird Watching

If Kayaking is not for you, Pacific Trails Resort does offer guided bird-watching trips.
Even though any time of day is great for Bird-Watching, our guided trips are always at sunset.
